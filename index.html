About Nelson

“I grew up in a society in which love stands above all else,in which
love is expected of mothers to their children and husbands to their
wives.”
I explore holistic themes in sculptures which broadly touch on love,Human beings need love animals too need love.
In 2002 i made a sculpture called The Nesting Chicks,in which the
chicks are craving for the love of their mother. The love between the
chicks and their mother is symbolic of the love expected between parents
and their children.
I also used themes that deals with the aspect of sharing feelings and
passing information. The elders in African society talks about family
issues and provide information often secretly to the young generation.In
2016 sharing secrets made of a very hard stone called Spring stone the
bird’s symbolises the wisdom of elderly on how to deal with bread and
butter issues that affect the families and the society at large. In most
my sculptures i use birds to tell my stories.

In 2006 i got enrolled at one of the local colleges and in 2007 i
graduated with a certificate in Horticulture. 2009 i got a job with
Prime Seed in partnership with USAID as Agricultural Field Officer.
For the entire time I was working there i was tormented by the
feeling and visions which I yearned to bring alive in stone. I became
morose to sculpt and unsociable and it even affected my performance at
work,  I then decided to quit and pick up my hammer and chisel and began
to sculpt again.
“ If I haven’t had time for my art I feel imprisoned and restricted. They are times that I feel like sculpting and others not.
Something forces or make me to just do it. I like to make things
which are strong and heavy with simple features,symbolic but not
complicated.
Sometimes my ideas come from the shape of the stone or as I work they
form under my fingers.Sometimes as I work ideas start to manifest and
remembered,feelings being recalled.All these go into the stone or come
out of the stone.
But however sometimes I have to express feelings and change the shape
of the stone completely or sometimes the stone can break after finding
an idea and then eventually everything is changed.Sometimes I feel like
my work is an adventure and don’t understand how it comes out or maybe
it’s because art is within me .
I am an Artist who tries to show people what I think feels or sees 
I want to bring an understanding of certain things to people. This
simply demonstrates how important Artist are within every society in
order for it to become whole.
